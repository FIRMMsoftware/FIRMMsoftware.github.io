FIRMM: Framewise Integrated Real-time MRI Monitoring

Easy to set up and use, the FIRMM software suite provides MRI scanner operators with head motion analytics in real time. Using FIRMM to identify the ideal scan time for each person can reduce total brain MRI scan times and associated costs by 50% or more.

Here's how FIRMM helps you improve data quality and reduce costs with real-time motion analytics.

Accurate FD calculations as you scan

FIRMM’s framewise displacement (FD) calculations are not only fast, but also accurate, when compared to a standard, commonly utilized offline, post-hoc processing stream.

Prediction of remaining scan time needed

We built an algorithm that accurately predicts the required scan time until you have captured as much low movement data as you need.

User friendly, real-time feedback

FIRMM users have been able to share the percentage of low-movement data frames with participants or display the FIRMM GUI on the participant’s screen in the scanner room for feedback and training purposes.
